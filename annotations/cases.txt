# Nominativ
Used when there is 1 person or 1 thing

---

# Akkusative
Used after specific `verbs` and `prepositions`.<br>
Used when there are 2 people or 2 things

### Example
- **Der hund** sieht **das kind**
- **Das Kind** sieht **den Hund**
- **Das kind** hat **einen Laptop**

---

# Dativ
Used after specific prepositions.
Also after specific verbs (50 verbs aproximately).
And finally after sentences with more than 1 Object.
